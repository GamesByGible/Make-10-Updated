How to Play

Combine all 4 numbers using + − × ÷ to land on exactly 10.

Drag one tile onto another to combine them in that order.

Or tap an operator first, then drag — it combines instantly using that operator.

Every puzzle is guaranteed solvable. Use ↺ to undo, ➜ for a new puzzle.
